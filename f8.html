About GITAM

GITAM (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management) is a well-known deemed-to-be university in India, with campuses in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru. Established in 1980 in Visakhapatnam, GITAM is recognized for its academic excellence and is one of the premier private institutions in India.

Programs Offered

GITAM offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in diverse fields such as:

  • Engineering & Technology: B.Tech., M.Tech.
  • Science: Biotechnology, Physics, Chemistry, and more.
  • Management: MBA, BBA.
  • Pharmacy: B.Pharm., M.Pharm.
  • Humanities: Law, International Business, Social Sciences.
  • Medical Sciences: BDS, MBBS, Nursing.

Campuses

GITAM has three campuses:

  • Visakhapatnam Campus: The main and oldest campus, located near the beach, offering modern infrastructure.
  • Hyderabad Campus: Located in Telangana, focusing on engineering, management, and law programs.
  • Bengaluru Campus: Known for high-quality education and strong industry connections.

Accreditations

GITAM is accredited with an ‘A+’ grade by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) and is recognized by UGC (University Grants Commission) and several professional bodies like AICTE, PCI, and MCI.

Facilities

GITAM offers state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped labs, libraries, hostels, and research centers. The campuses also have excellent sports facilities and clubs for extracurricular activities.

Student Life

GITAM is known for its vibrant campus life with cultural fests, technical events, and student clubs. Popular events like the GUSAC Carnival and Pramana (annual tech fest) bring students together for fun and learning. The institution encourages holistic development through academics and extracurricular activities.

Focus on Research

GITAM promotes research through collaborations with industries and institutions. It provides scholarships and funding for innovative research projects, fostering a strong research culture among its students and faculty.
